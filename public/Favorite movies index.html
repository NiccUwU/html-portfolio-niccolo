The Best Movies According to Niccolo

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Shutter Island

A mind bending suspense with a bone-chilling plot twist

The Dark Knight

Batman and Joker; polar opposites both in character and in style, the perfect rivals; both trying to outsmart each other as friends and loved ones are caught in the crossfires of their battles

Bo Burnham: Inside

A relatable tragedy about the lonely living conditions in one of the most pivotal moments in recent history, the COVID-19 pandemic; all narrated in a mix of catchy music and toilet humor
